About Lexocrates

Leading legal process outsourcing company with 15+ years of excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Lexocrates Legal Services Pvt Ltd began with a simple yet powerful vision: to make high-quality legal services accessible to businesses worldwide through innovative outsourcing solutions.

What started as a small team of dedicated legal professionals has grown into a leading legal process outsourcing company, serving over 500 clients across 25+ countries. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, unwavering commitment to quality, and deep understanding of our clients' evolving legal needs.

Today, we stand as a trusted partner for corporations, law firms, and businesses seeking efficient, cost-effective, and reliable legal support services. Our success is built on the foundation of expertise, integrity, and client satisfaction.
