Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our legal process outsourcing services

What jurisdictions do you cover?

We provide legal support across multiple jurisdictions including US (all states), UK, Canada, Australia, and Commonwealth countries. Our team is trained in international legal systems and can handle cross-border legal matters with expertise in local regulations and global frameworks.

How do you ensure data confidentiality?

We maintain enterprise-grade security with ISO 27001 certification, end-to-end encryption, secure VPN access, two-factor authentication, and strict confidentiality protocols. All team members sign comprehensive NDAs and undergo regular security training.

Can you work with our existing tools and workflow?

Absolutely! We integrate seamlessly with your existing legal technology stack including Westlaw, LexisNexis, Relativity, Clio, and other industry-standard tools. We adapt to your workflow preferences and can work within your established processes.

Are your lawyers trained in US/Commonwealth legal systems?

Yes, our legal professionals receive specialized training in US and Commonwealth legal systems. Many have additional certifications and ongoing education in international law, ensuring they stay current with legal developments across jurisdictions.

What is your typical response time for urgent matters?

We offer 24/7 support for urgent matters with response times as quick as 2-4 hours for critical issues. Our flexible working model ensures we can accommodate time-sensitive deadlines across different time zones.

How do you ensure quality control?

We implement a multi-tier quality control process including peer review, senior attorney oversight, and automated quality checks. Every deliverable undergoes multiple review stages to maintain our high standards of excellence.

What are your pricing models?

We offer flexible engagement models including hourly rates, per-document pricing, full-time equivalent (FTE) arrangements, and project-based pricing. We work with you to find the most cost-effective solution for your specific needs.

How do you handle conflicts of interest?

We conduct thorough conflict checks before accepting any new client or matter. Our conflict management system tracks all client relationships and ensures we maintain strict ethical standards and client confidentiality.
